Kandy

Inroduction to Kandy

Once you visit the big town of Kandy, a must visit place is the “Temple of the Tooth”. This was built back in 1595 and was used as a kingdom. This is currently used as a temple of worship for the locals and the tourists. The reason this is very important is because this currently holds the “sacred tooth relic of Lord Buddha”. This temple is visited by many religious people since Sri Lanka also holds many other Buddhist related history.

The temple used to be a kingdom back in the day, meaning the architecture of this temple is fabulous and photogenic from every end. You can also see the well-built defense system and a waterbed covering around the temple.

You can see the Kandy Lake right next to the temple. This lake is famous for having it in the middle of a very densely populated city. A few kms away you can find the “Royal Botanic Gardens” which is also a very greenly place where u can keep your mind in peace after a long trip. A garden filled with pretty flowers and cute birds humming around the garden. A must visit once in Kandy.

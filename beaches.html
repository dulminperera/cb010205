Beaches

Pasikuda

Pasikuda translates to “green algae bay” because of the sea green color sea water in the area. Pasikuda beach is located 35 km onto the northwestern side of Sri Lankan in Batticlo. This is famous to have one the calmest waves in Sri Lanka. The ocean is vey swallow that u can walk to into the sea for several minutes and several feet away from the coast and still have the water level under your knee. This is also not a vey crowded beach and can also be called a hidden gem because not many people know the place. The environment is indeed a very scenic view with not so harsh sunlight.

Hotel: www.booking.com

Unawatuna

Unawatuna is one of the most attractive beaches in Sri Lanka in a town in Galle. The climate is warm but no too sunny, indeed very windy meaning it is a good place to relax and spend your evening. The waves are strong not enough to surf with but not strong that you can’t swim in it. A beach full of fun water activities and resorts all along the beach so any tourists can have a well-lit dinner with sea waves only a few feet away. The two words “Fantastic”, and “Scenic” are understatements when it comes to the sunset.

Hotel: www.booking.com
